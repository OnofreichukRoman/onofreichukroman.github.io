# Meme Soundboard

Meme Soundboard is a lightweight Android app for playing short, meme-style sound effects. It is designed for quick offline taps, simple browsing, and fun reactions.

## App Summary

Meme Soundboard gives users a fast collection of sound clips grouped by categories such as brainrot, sigma, NPC, rage, cursed, bass, and TTS-style reactions. Users can search sounds, mark favorites, view recently played clips, and share the app with friends.

The app is built as a simple mobile entertainment tool.

## Key Features

- One-tap meme sound playback
- Offline local sound library
- Search by title, category, and tags
- Category filters for quick browsing
- Favorites for saving preferred sounds
- Recently played sounds
- Haptic feedback for button taps
- Share option for recommending the app

## Content Policy

Meme Soundboard is intended to avoid copyrighted or misleading audio content. The app should not include:

- Movie, TV, anime, or game audio rips
- TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, or streamer clips without permission
- Copyrighted music samples
- Celebrity voice clones
- Brand-specific or franchise-owned sounds

Acceptable content includes:

- Original recorded sounds
- Original generated effects
- Original TTS-style phrases
- Public-domain sounds
- CC0 or royalty-free sounds that allow app distribution
- Properly licensed clips with documented usage rights

## Privacy Overview

Meme Soundboard is designed to work without user accounts. The core soundboard experience uses local app data for favorites and recently played sounds.

The app may use third-party services such as Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, or related Google services when production configuration is enabled. These services may collect device, advertising, diagnostics, or usage data according to their own privacy policies and the app's published privacy policy.

## Intended Audience

Meme Soundboard is intended for general entertainment users who enjoy short reaction sounds and meme-style audio effects. The app should remain appropriate for broad audiences by avoiding explicit, hateful, harassing, copyrighted, or unsafe audio content.
