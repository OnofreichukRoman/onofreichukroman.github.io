Scope

This privacy policy covers the developer website hosted on GitHub Pages and the Android app Meme Soundboard. It explains what data is collected, why it is collected, and how it is used.

Data Collection

Local app data: Meme Soundboard stores user preferences locally, such as favorites and recently played sounds. No user account is required.

Device data: The app may use standard device identifiers and analytics data if third-party services are enabled, such as Google Firebase Analytics or Google AdMob.

Third-Party Services

Meme Soundboard may use third-party services for analytics, advertising, or crash reporting. These services may collect data independently according to their own privacy policies.

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google AdMob
  • Other Google services related to app performance or advertising

Use of Data

Data is used to improve application performance, deliver relevant ads where applicable, and identify technical issues. Local app data ensures a better user experience without requiring login credentials.

Data Sharing

Data is not sold. Third-party providers may process analytics or advertising data on behalf of the developer according to their policies. This privacy policy does not cover third-party service providers.

Children and Sensitive Content

The app is intended for general audiences and is designed to avoid explicit, hateful, harassing, or infringing content. It is the developer's intent to maintain a safe experience.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as new apps are added or service configurations change. The effective date is the date this page is published.
